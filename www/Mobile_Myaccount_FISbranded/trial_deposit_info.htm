Trial Deposit

- Information

Here is what to expect:

1. Two small deposits will be made into the account within 3-5 banking days.

2. Two debits in the same amounts as the credits will also be made to the same account.

3. In 3-5 business days, check your bank account to obtain the exact amount of each deposit. Be sure to write the amounts down for reference. The description should say 'Bank Acct Trial Deposit' or a similar description.

4. The last step will be to log back into the MyAccount site to enter the amounts.

5. If the amounts you enter match the deposits, account ownership is verified and your bank account will then be eligible as a funding source.
