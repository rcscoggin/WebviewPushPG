Frequently Asked Questions

 

Q: Is the Card a credit card?

A: No. The Card is a prepaid MasterCard/Visa card that carries a fixed cash amount determined by the giver at the time of purchase. Each time the gift recipient uses the card, the purchase amount is deducted from the available balance.
[ Top ]

Q: Where can the Card be used?

A: The Card can be used to make purchases everywhere MasterCard/Visa is accepted, at more than 19 million MasterCard/Visa merchant locations.
[ Top ]

Q: Who might like a Card?

A: Anyone. Friends, relatives, business associates. It's a gift that can be used everywhere MasterCard/Visa is accepted.
[ Top ]

Q: How much value the Card can have?

A: The minimum value the Card can have is $25 and the maximum value is $250.
[ Top ]

Q: Who can order or use the card?

A: Anyone can order the card through this web site at www.mybank.com, or by phone at 1-877-555-5555.
[ Top ]

Q: What are the fees to buy the Card?

A: The Shipping and Handling fee will be waived for new card orders through February 28, 2001. The Shipping and Handling fee beginning March 1, 2001 will be $6.00 per card.
[ Top ]

Q: When does the gift recipient receive the card?

A: All cards are mailed the next business day, as long as the order is placed before 4:30 PM EST. Typically, the gift recipient should receive the card within five to seven business days after the order was placed. The card is delivered with a carrier that includes information on who sent the card, the greeting message and the card value amount.
[ Top ]

Q: Does the gift recipient need to sign an agreement?

A: Each card is distributed with a Cardholder Agreement & Disclosure. This agreement fully explains the terms and conditions regarding the use of the card. This agreement can also be viewed online. The gift recipient accepts the terms and conditions by either signing the back of the card, using the card, or receiving the card by request.
[ Top ]

Q: Will the gift recipient receive a statement?

A: Gift recipients can review their current balance online free of charge through this Web site. In addition, the gift recipient can call the toll-free Cardholder Services number 1-866-555-5555 to receive balance and transaction information.
[ Top ]

Q: What happens if I need to return an item?

A: Each merchant location has its own return policies and will handle the returns in the same manner as any other MasterCard/Visa transaction. They may credit your Card, provide a cash refund, or a store credit.
[ Top ]

Q: What if the purchase is denied?

A: The card´s available balance is probably less than the purchase amount. You may inform the merchant of the remaining balance on the card and use another form of payment to pay the difference, subject to the policy of the merchant. Also, please be aware that use of the Card at some merchants for example, restaurants, mail-order companies, and cruise lines requires the card have an available balance greater than the purchase amount to ensure sufficient funds for tips or incidental expenses. Use of the card at automated fuel dispensers requires a minimum balance of $30. However, only the actual amount spent will be deducted from the card.
[ Top ]

Q: Does the Card have an expiration date?

A: Yes. It expires 12 months from the purchase date, as indicated on the front of the card.
[ Top ]

Q: Can I reload my Card?

A: No, the card cannot be reloaded.
[ Top ]

Q: What happens if there are still funds available when my Card expires?

A: We encourage you to use of the entire gift amount before the card expires. There's a $15 administrative fee to close out the account (or the remaining balance if it's less than $15). Any remaining balance would be returned to the recipient by check within four to six weeks.
[ Top ]

Q: What happens if my Card is lost or stolen?

A: MyBank provides 24-hour toll-free support. Immediately report any problems or a lost or stolen card by calling 1-866-555-5555. A replacement card will be issued and any available balance will be transferred to the new card, less a $6.00 replacement card fee that will be deducted from the card´s available balance. As long as the lost or stolen card is reported immediately, you will not be responsible for any unauthorized merchant charges.
[ Top ]

Q: Can the card ever have a negative balance?

A: Any authorization request that is greater than the card's available balance will be declined. However, there can be times when a MasterCard/Visa merchant puts an item through without prior authorization. If an overdraft occurs, the card recipient will be required to make a payment to cardholder services to cover the negative amount.
